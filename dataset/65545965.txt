Mërgim Kida (Albanian: [məɾgim kida]; born 7 August 1997), known professionally as Kidda, is a Kosovo-Albanian rapper and songwriter.


== Life and career ==
Mërgim Kida was born on 7 August 1997 into an Albanian family from Gjakovë, Kosovo, in the city of Gävle, Sweden.
