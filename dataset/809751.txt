Fumiko Orikasa (折笠 富美子, Orikasa Fumiko, born December 27, 1974) is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer. She has been the voice of Meyrin Hawke in Mobile Suit Gundam Seed Destiny, Chun-Li in Street Fighter, Kanade Minamino/Cure Rhythm in Suite PreCure, Lotte Yanson in Little Witch Academia and Riza Hawkeye in Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood.
