Park Yun-ha (Korean: 박윤하; born April 23, 1991), better known by her stage name Giant Pink, is a South Korean rapper. She was the winner of hip-hop competition show Unpretty Rapstar 3, and she is currently a member of the hip-hop label AIKM (All I Know Music) under SM Entertainment.


== Personal life ==
Giant Pink married a younger businessman in November 2020. On December 6, 2021, she announced that she is pregnant after a year of marriage.
