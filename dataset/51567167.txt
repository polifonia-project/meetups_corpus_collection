Taiga Nakano (仲野 太賀, Nakano Taiga, born February 7, 1993, in Tokyo, Japan) is a Japanese actor. He is represented with Stardust Promotion. He is the second son of actor Hideo Nakano. He was previously credited as only Taiga.
