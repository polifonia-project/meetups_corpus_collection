Mai Nakahara (中原 麻衣, Nakahara Mai, born February 23, 1981) is a Japanese voice actress and singer associated with I'm Enterprise. She played Rena Ryūgū in Higurashi When They Cry, Nagisa Furukawa in Clannad, Seele in Honkai Impact 3rd, and Akagi in Azur Lane. She performed theme songs for each series, some of which have charted on Oricon.
