Alexander Mikhailovich Anissimov (Анисимов, Александр Михайлович) (born 8 October 1947) is a Russian  conductor.In 1995 he was appointed Principal Guest Conductor of the National Symphony Orchestra of Ireland.


== Selected Recordings ==
As conductor:

Rachmaninoff: Complete symphonies, choral symphony The Bells, symphonic poems. NSOI. Naxos.
Glazunov: Complete symphonies, ballets, and symphonic poems. MSO. Naxos.
