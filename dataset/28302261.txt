Rich Mullins is the former bassist of the alternative, desert rock band Karma To Burn, Speedealer (originally REO Speedealer before lawsuit), and the rock band Year Long Disaster, both of which shared the bandmate Daniel Davies. He is known for his slouched gait whilst playing his bass.
