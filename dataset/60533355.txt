Anne Amie is an Indian playback singer. Her career began when she sang her first solo, "Ethu Meghamari", in the film Kochavva Paulo Ayyappa Coelho.


== Career ==
She participated in the music reality show, Star Singer on Asianet. Subsequently, she got a chance to work with Shaan Rahman for the film Kochavva Paulo Ayyappa Coelho. However, that song was not included in the movie. She has also worked with known music directors like M. Jayachandran, Bijibal, Gopi Sunder, Alphons Joseph, Ratheesh Vega, Sushin Shyam, Mejo Joseph and Ifthi.
