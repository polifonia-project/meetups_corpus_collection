Isiah Pinkney, known under the stage name 12 Gauge, is an American rapper from Georgia.He started out as a DJ, moving to rapping in the early 1990s, releasing three full-length albums. He is best known for his only top 40 single "Dunkie Butt", which peaked at 28 on Billboard Hot 100 and earned a gold certification.
