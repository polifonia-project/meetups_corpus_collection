Askjell Solstrand, also known mononymously as Askjell, is a Norwegian record producer and songwriter, currently based in Bergen, Norway. He has grown as an affiliate with acts, including Sigrid, Aurora and Iris. He has also released two extended plays, entitled Requiem and To Be Loved, as a solo artist in 2019. His debut album, everything will be ok, is slated for release in early 2022.
