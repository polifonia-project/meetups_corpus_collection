Michael Moloney (born 15 November 1944) is a traditional Irish musician and scholar.
In 1992, Moloney received a Ph.D. in Folklore and Folklife from the University of Pennsylvania. For his work in public folklore, he received a 1999 National Heritage Fellowship from the National Endowment for the Arts.


== Awards ==
1999 National Heritage Fellowship
2000 Pew Fellowships in the Arts
2013 Presidential Distinguished Service Awards for the Irish Abroad
