Manfred Preis (born 1954, in Hengersberg) is a German bass clarinetist and saxophonist.
Preis studied at the Munich Musikhochschule and the Orchestra Academy of the Berliner Philharmoniker. He played clarinet in the Berlin Radio Symphony Orchestra.
