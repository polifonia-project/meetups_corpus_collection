Peter J. Devlin is an American sound engineer. He has been nominated for five Academy Awards in the category Best Sound. He has worked on over 50 films since 1988.


== Selected filmography ==
Pearl Harbor (2001)
Transformers (2007)
Star Trek (2009)
Transformers: Dark of the Moon (2011)
Black Panther (2018)
