Dabbu (Bengali: ডাব্বু)(Real name: Prasenjit Ghosal)  is an Indian Bengali film music composer whose songs have been featured in Bengali movies. Known as ‘’Dabbu" in the national music scene, he is a composer, guitarist and music arranger.


== Performance ==
Performed with ‘’This is not fusion’’ project across the world.


== Career ==
Dabbu started his career as a music composer with Shree Venkatesh Films in Kolkata. He has also been a background music director. Some of his projects include Bangali Babu English Mem and Jamai 420.
