Radiša Trajković  (Serbian Cyrillic: Радиша Трајковић; born 23 July 1973), known by the stage name Đani (Serbian Cyrillic: Ђани), is a popular Serbian pop-folk singer. He was born in Lipljan and attended school there.He is married to Slađana. He is stepfather to her son from her previous marriage.
