Fedde Le Grand (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈfɛdə lə ɡrɑnt]; born 7 September 1977) is a Dutch house DJ, record producer and remixer from Utrecht.


== Biography ==
In 2006, his single "Put Your Hands Up For Detroit" (UK/US) / "Put Your Hands Up 4 Detroit" (Europe, excluding the UK) reached the top five on the Dutch Top 40, number one in the UK Singles Chart and spent five weeks on the dance charts in Spain.
