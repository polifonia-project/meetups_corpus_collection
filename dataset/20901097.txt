Rin Takanashi (高梨 臨, Takanashi Rin, born December 17, 1988) is a Japanese film and television actress.


== Career ==
Takanashi starred in the 2007 film adaptation of the Japanese novel Goth as one of the lead characters, Yoru Morino. She starred in the 2009 Super Sentai series Samurai Sentai Shinkenger as one of the lead characters, Mako Shiraishi/Shinken Pink.  She starred in Kamen Rider W Returns in July 2011. Rin Takanashi is part of the gravure idol group, Pink Jam Princess, with four others.She co-starred with Ryo Kase in Abbas Kiarostami's 2012 film Like Someone in Love.
