Jung Chan-woo (Korean: 정찬우; Hanja: 鄭燦宇; born February 28, 1968), is a South Korean comedian, actor and singer. He is member of comedy duo Cultwo. His syndicated talk radio show Cultwo Show, airs via the SBS Power FM since 2006.
