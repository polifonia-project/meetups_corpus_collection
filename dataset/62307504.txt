Taner Ölmez (born 9 August 1986) is a Turkish actor who started his acting career in 2009. His first drama was Kayıp Şehir where he acted as Sadık. He gained fame through Medcezir in which he played Mert Asım Serez, and is also known for portraying Ali Vefa in Mucize Doktor. He the lead singer of the barabar group. He married with Ece Çesmioglu june 2021.
