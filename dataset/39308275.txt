Snežana Babić (Serbian Cyrillic: Снежана Бабић Снеки; born 1 October 1967), better known as Sneki, is a Serbian singer and actress.
She appeared in the first season of the Serbian version of Your Face Sounds Familiar.
Sneki famously insured her 1.25 m (4 ft 1 in) long legs at one million euros each.
