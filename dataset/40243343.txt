Sumire Morohoshi (諸星 すみれ, Morohoshi Sumire, born April 23, 1999) is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer from Kanagawa Prefecture. She is affiliated with Himawari Theatre Group. She debuted as a solo singer under the record label, Flying Dog.


== Biography ==
Born in Kanagawa Prefecture in 1999, she was born with the right side of her body paralysed. The paralysis was cured in the first few months of her life.She joined the theater company Sunflower at the age of three. One of the reasons for joining the group was that she longed for the character Yukino in Spirited Away and said to her parents that she wanted to become Yubaba.
In 2006, at the age of seven, she voiced Carrie in the anime series Red Garden and was shocked by the performance of Takehito Koyasu that she co-starred. She said, "She was overwhelmed to change the atmosphere in the voice."
In 2009, at the age of nine, Morohoshi voiced Nina Tucker in Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood and the staff and co-stars evaluated that her "acting is skillful."
She was chosen as Betty in Heroman because the staff knew the voices of various stars as Nina and thought to work together someday.
Since 2009, she has been active as a voice actor, but she also performs in television dramas and on stage as an actress. In the fall of 2019, debuted as a singer from the record label FlyingDog with the track "Pure White", which was used for the opening theme to Ascendance of a Bookworm, and released as part of her debut album Smile. Six months later, her first solo single "Tsumujikaze" was released as the opening theme to the second season of Ascendance of a Bookworm.
