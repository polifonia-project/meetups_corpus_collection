Saeko Zōgō (藏合 紗恵子, Zōgō Saeko, born August 13, 1984) is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer from Tsuyama, Okayama Prefecture, Japan. She is affiliated with Production Ace. Her major roles are: Ichiyon (M14) in Upotte!!, Nadeshiko Sōma in Isuca, Freon Flamel in Sky Wizards Academy, Sōji Okita in Dai-Shogun - Great Revolution, and Shiori Itsuka in Date A Live II. She and fellow voice actress Kaori Sadohara were part of a duo called Two-Formula who sang songs on Isuca and Noucome. She announced her marriage on May 21, 2017.
