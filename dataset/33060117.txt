Misuzu Togashi (富樫 美鈴, Togashi Misuzu, born August 7) is a Japanese actress, voice-actress, and singer affiliated with Ken Production. She is known for Minakami Mai in Nichijou, Amano Yukiteru in Mirai-Nikki, and Tobiichi Origami in the Date A Live-series.
