Müge Anlı (born 19 December 1973) is a Turkish television presenter and journalist.After presenting the news magazine program Dobra Dobra, she started her own discussion program, Müge Anlı ile Tatlı Sert, on ATV. In the program, people who are looking for their lost relatives are welcomed, and issues such as unsolved murders and lost victims who cannot be found are discussed.


== Personal life ==
Anlı is of Albanian descent. In 2008, she divorced her husband, journalist Burhan Akdağ. Together they have a daughter.
