Guy Roche is a record producer and songwriter who is best known for the hit singles he has co-written/co-produced for Christina Aguilera, which are "What a Girl Wants", "I Turn to You", and "Come On Over Baby (All I Want Is You)". He also co-wrote Brandy's Never Say Never single "Almost Doesn't Count" (along with Shelly Peiken). He also produced Selena's song "Dreaming of You", included on Selena's last album Dreaming of You.
