Charumathi Ramachandran (born 12 July 1951) is a Carnatic music singer.
She is a disciple of M.L. Vasanthakumari. She was a gold medalist and stood first in music from the Madras University. She was one of the first carnatic vocalists to introduce Hindustani forms in her music.She is married to Trichur V. Ramachandran, who is also a Carnatic vocalist.
