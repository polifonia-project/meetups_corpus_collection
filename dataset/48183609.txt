Kim Yong-jun (Korean: 김용준; born September 12, 1984) is a South Korean singer. He is a member of the band SG Wannabe. In 2009, he and then-real life girlfriend Hwang Jung-eum joined the second season of reality dating show We Got Married as the first real-life couple to be featured on the show.
