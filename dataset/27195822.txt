Jose "Boboy" Teves Garrovillo Jr. (born 10 October 1951) is a Filipino singer and actor known for being a member of the musical trio APO Hiking Society. He has performed with the APO Hiking Society in almost all major cities in the Philippines, apart from holding a series of concerts in Asia, Middle East, United States, Canada, and Europe.


== Family life ==
Garrovillo was born October 10, 1951 to Jose Garrovillo Sr. and Paulita Teves, sister of Senator Lorenzo Teves and aunt of former Secretary of Finance Margarito Teves. He is married to Elizabeth "Bong" Agcaoili and have two sons, Alfonso and Antonio.
