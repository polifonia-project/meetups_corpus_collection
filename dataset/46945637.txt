Yuuka Yano (矢野 優花, Yano Yūka, born January 6, 1998, in Miyazaki Prefecture) is a Japanese actress. She played the role of Fuka Igasaki (Shiro Ninger) in the 2015 Super Sentai TV series Shuriken Sentai Ninninger.


== Biography ==
In 2014, Yano won the  1st Uniforms Women & Men's Contest modelling competition.In 2015, Yano acted in her first television role as Fuka Igasaki/Shironinger in the 39th Super Sentai series, Shuriken Sentai Ninninger.
