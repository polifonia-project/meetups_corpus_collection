Nick Holywell-Walker is a British musician best known as the stage keyboard player for English post-punk band Killing Joke, beginning in 1994. He left in 2004 to concentrate on his own projects. A singer, songwriter, producer and composer, he resides in North London, England. He is founding member of UK band Wandering Crow and a father of three.
Since 1998 he has also composed, produced and mixed soundtracks for film + TV extensively.
