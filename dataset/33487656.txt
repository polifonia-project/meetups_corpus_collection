Doug Hemphill is an American sound mixer. He won an Academy Award for Best Sound and has been nominated for nine more in the same category. He has worked on more than 190 films since 1979.


== Selected filmography ==
Hemphill has won an Academy Award for Best Sound and has been nominated for another nine:
