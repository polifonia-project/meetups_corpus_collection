Clara Hätzlerin (c. 1430 – 1476) was a professional scribe in 15th century Augsburg. Her 1471 Liederbuch (songbook) was among the sources used by Carl Orff for his Die Bernauerin.Clara-Hätzler-Straße in Augsburg is named after her.
