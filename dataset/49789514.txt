Houkago Princess (放課後プリンセス, Hōkago Purinsesu, lit. "After School Princess") is a Japanese idol girl group. Their album Seifuku Cinderella reached the sixth place on the weekly Oricon Albums Chart and their single "Junpaku Antoinette" reached the second place on the weekly Oricon Singles Chart.
