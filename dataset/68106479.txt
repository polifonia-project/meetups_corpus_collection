Gary E. Grant is an American trumpet player, composer and producer, and session musician who has played on hundreds of commercial recordings.


== Biography ==
Grant attended North Texas State University and toured with Woody Herman band, where he performed as a lead trumpet player and soloist.In 1975, he moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career as a full time musician.
Grant has recorded with producer Quincy Jones on albums Back On The Block and Q's Juke Joint.
Grant has been a long time member of the Jerry Hey horn section The Jerry Hey Horns.
