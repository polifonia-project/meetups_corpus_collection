Manisha Eerabathini is an Indian-American playback singer who works in the Telugu film industry.


== Career ==
Her 2017 song "Arere Yekkada" was a major hit and brought recognition to her name and talent.In 2018, she sang with Roll Rida in the song "Arupu," released as a music video. The song's message and depiction is about the abuse against women. It received critical acclaim.
