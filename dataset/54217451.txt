Luri (born September 5, 1996), also known as Lee Su-hyun, is a South Korean singer and actress. She is known as a former contestant of Produce 101 and as a member of the girl group I.B.I, which debuted in 2016.In 2017, she became a co-host of Tooniverse's Stress Out. In the same year, she portrayed Lee Ha-ra in the drama Happy Talk as her first major leading role.In 2019, Lee Su-hyun changed her stage name to Luri (루리) in preparation of a solo debut.
