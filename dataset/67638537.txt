Haris Džinović (born 26 September 1951) is a Bosnian and former Yugoslavian folk singer, composer and lyricist.Džinović is one of the best known singers of Southeastern Europe, having been active on the music scene since 1981, when he founded his own group Sar e Roma, with which he recorded three albums from 1982 to 1985. He has been a solo singer since 1989.
