Im Na-yeon (Korean: 임나연; born September 22, 1995), known mononymously as Nayeon, is a South Korean singer. After participating on the reality survival television show Sixteen in 2015, she became a member in the South Korean girl group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment. She is the oldest member of Twice.


== Life and career ==


=== Early life ===
As a child, she had participated in a child modeling contest and was eventually cast by idol agency JYP Entertainment. However, her mother prevented her from starting training and entering show business right away given her young age. On September 15, 2010, she secretly attended JYP Entertainment's 7th Open Audition after which she became a trainee. In 2013, she was initially cast as a member of 6mix, a JYP girl group that was planned but never debuted. She also made an appearance in the second episode of KBS2's 2012 Korean drama Dream High 2.


=== 2015–present: Sixteen and Twice ===

In 2015, Nayeon participated in the reality television show Sixteen, a competition designed to choose the founding members of Twice. As one of the nine successful participants, she went on to join the newly formed girl group Twice.In October 2015, Nayeon officially debuted as a member of Twice with their first extended play, The Story Begins. In Gallup Korea's annual music poll for 2017, Nayeon was voted the sixth most popular idol in South Korea, ahead of all her bandmates. In the 2018 poll, she was again ranked sixth, receiving 6.7% of the votes. In 2019, she was ranked fifth, receiving 8.2% of the votes. In 2019, Nayeon was also ranked as the eighth most popular female K-pop idol in a survey of soldiers doing mandatory military service in South Korea.


== Other ventures ==


=== Endorsements ===
Aside from various endorsements with her bandmates, Nayeon has done work promoting various products and featured in several advertisements. During trainee years, Nayeon became a model for brands such as Smart School Uniform and TN.In May 2021, she became a model and appeared in the promotional video for Chaumet's Joséphine Collection. In August, she became a model for Biotherm Korea, a South Korean beauty brand. Nayeon also appeared as a promotional model for magazines, including Nylon Korea, Cosmopolitan Korea, W Korea, and Elle Korea for various fashion brand in South Korea such as Ralph Lauren, Jill Stuart, Fendi, and Louis Vuitton.
