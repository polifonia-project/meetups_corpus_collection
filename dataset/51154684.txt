Nathan King (born 29 August 1970 from the Isle of Wight) is an English rock musician who has played in three notable bands: Level 42, Frost* and It Bites. He is the younger brother of Level 42's Mark King.
Recently, King has played guitar and bass with The Blockheads.King is also a contributor to the 'All About The Bass' series on the Andertons TV YouTube channel.
