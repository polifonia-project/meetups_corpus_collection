Johan Ernst Hartmann  (2 March 1770 – 16 December 1844) was a Danish organist and composer, and son of composer and violinist Johann Hartmann. In 1795, he started as an organist at Frederik's German Church in Christianshavn. In 1807, he became cantor at the Roskilde Cathedral and died December 16, 1844. Some of his compositions, including cantatas, were performed in the years 1789-97. At Roskilde, he established a renowned choir for which he composed various works .His son, Søren Hartmann (1815-1912) became his successor as cantor at Roskilde Cathedral, where he also stayed for 40 years until 1883.
