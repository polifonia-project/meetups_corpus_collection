Eva Turnová (born 1964) is a Czech singer, songwriter, bass guitarist, teacher, translator and actress. Since 2001 she was a member of The Plastic People of the Universe. She left the band in 2015. Between 1995 and 2001 she was a member of DG 307. Her first husband was Czech actor David Matásek (divorced five years after the wedding). In 2008, she released first solo album called Eturnity.
