Nikola Rokvić (Serbian Cyrillic: Никола Роквић; born 29 June 1985) is a Serbian singer and entrepreneur. The oldest son of folk singer Marinko Rokvić, he also became a well-known artist upon the release of his 2006 debut album, titled Oprosti mojoj mladosti. Although primarily known for performing pop-folk music, Rokvić also ventured into pop since the release of Ovaj put in 2015.Beyond his music career, Rokvić participated in the reality TV shows Survivor Srbija VIP: Philippines (2010) and the third season of Serbian spin-off of Your Face Sounds Familiar (2016), where he finished as the runner-up. He is also a co-founder and CEO of the first Serbian streaming platform, called YouBox.During his time in the Philippines, Rokvić developed an on-screen romantic relationship with fellow contestant, Serbian-American model Bojana Barović. The couple eventually married in 2016, and they have two children.
