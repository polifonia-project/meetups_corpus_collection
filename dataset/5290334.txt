Vera Matović (Serbian Cyrillic: Вера Матовић, born June 3, 1946, in Čačak) is a Serbian folk singer. Her career started in 1974 with the release of her first album, Pomiri se sudbinom; she has since released 26 albums and 13 singles. She finished Economical secondary school. During primary school, she sang with her brother's orchestra in Čačak.
