Flederman is an Australian contemporary music ensemble co-founded by Carl Vine and Simone de Haan. Their selftitled album won the 1989 ARIA Award for Best Classical Album was nominated for Best Independent Release.


== Members ==
Geoffrey Collins, flute
David Pereira, cello
Brett Kelly, trombone
David Miller, piano
Carl Vine, keyboards
Graeme Leak, percussion
Michael Askill, percussion
Simone de Haan, trombone
