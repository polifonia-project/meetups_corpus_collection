Michael Sean McCary (born December 16, 1971) is an American singer, known for being a former bass singer of the R&B group Boyz II Men (in which he was sometimes known as Mike Bass).In 2003, McCary left Boyz II Men due to back spasms that were eventually diagnosed as multiple sclerosis. He was shown in the pilot for a talk show with Paula Poundstone called A Pound of Paula, which wasn't picked up.
