Stuart John Wood (born 25 February 1957) is a Scottish musician, songwriter and producer best known as a guitarist for the 1970s band the Bay City Rollers.He remains active touring the world with his new generation Bay City Rollers and also behind the scenes in the music industry, producing music through The Music Kitchen.In December 2019 Wood rescued Kruger, a 10-year-old black Labrador, which had gone missing in North Berwick, East Lothian.
