William Francis Karren is an American musician, best known as the lead guitarist of the punk/riot grrrl band Bikini Kill, formed by Kathleen Hanna, with Karren, Tobi Vail and Kathi Wilcox. He was also active in many other music projects including the Go Team, the Frumpies, Corrections, and Spray Painted Love (with Tobi Vail). In 2019 he did not participate in Bikini Kill's reunion his replacement being Erica Dawn Lyle.
In a Bikini Kill interview, Karren gave his influences as the Slits, the Ronettes, Chrissie Hynde and Wire.
