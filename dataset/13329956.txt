Rekka Katakiri (片霧烈火, Katakiri Rekka, born December 8) is a female Japanese singer under the self-published label Closed/Underground. She has released several songs that have served as theme songs for anime shows, including Higurashi When They Cry, My Wife is the Student Council President, Shin Koihime Muso, and Kokoro Connect.
