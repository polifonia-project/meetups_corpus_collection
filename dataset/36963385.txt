Stella Chung (Chinese: 钟晓玉; pinyin: Zhōng Xiǎoyù, born 9 July 1981) is a Malaysian actress and singer.


== Career ==
Stella started her entertainment career by making her debut as the leading actress in a local RTM Chinese drama. She then signed to Vertex Music in 2006 and released her first studio album in 2007. She has also starred in a number of Singaporean–Malaysian-co-produced Chinese dramas.


== Personal life ==
Chung was born in Sarawak and spent her schooling years in Sarikei. Her older brother, Nick Chung (Chinese: 钟盛忠), is also a singer.
