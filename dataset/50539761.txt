Michiru Sasano (笹野 みちる, Sasano Michiru, born 30 October 1967), is a Japanese pop singer and songwriter. In 1988, when she was attending college in Doshisha University, a Japanese pop band Tokyo Shōnen was born, where she became the songwriter and vocalist. After the band broke up, she became a solo artist in 1993.
