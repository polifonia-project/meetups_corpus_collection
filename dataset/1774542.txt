Svetlana Lvovna Geiman (Russian: Светлана Львовна Гейман; born 29 April 1975), known professionally as Linda (Russian: Линда), is a Russian singer and songwriter. Her style incorporates trip hop, electronic, and ethnic music. Since January 2012, she has been married to Greek composer Stefanos Korkolis.
