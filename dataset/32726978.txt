Dean Magraw is an American guitarist and composer.


== Biography ==
Magraw was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota and grew up in St. Paul. He began playing guitar at the age of 13. Magraw performed in a duo for many years with Peter Ostroushko and has also performed with Tim Sparks, John Gorka and many other artists.His musical spectrum ranges from world music band Boiled in Lead to jazz band Red Planet.
