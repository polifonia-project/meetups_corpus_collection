Park Ji-bin (Korean: 박지빈; born March 14, 1995) is a South Korean actor. He began his career as a child actor, notably in Hello, Brother (2005) and Boys Over Flowers (2009) Park enlisted in the military on May 26, 2015 and was discharged on February 25, 2017.
