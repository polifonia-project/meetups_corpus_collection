Tomoe Ohmi (近江 知永, Ōmi Tomoe, born October 13, 1982) is a Japanese singer and voice actress. She received training from the Japan Narration Acting Institute (日本ナレーション演技研究所, Nippon Narēshon Engi Kenkyūjo) and RAMS Professional Education. She made her debut as a voice actress in the anime television series School Rumble.
