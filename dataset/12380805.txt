Jonas Quant, also known simply as Quant, is a Swedish record producer, songwriter and remixer from Gothenburg.In 2010, Quant co-produced English duo Hurts' debut album Happiness, which became the fastest-selling debut album of that year by a band in the United Kingdom. Quant has also collaborated with artists such as No Doubt, Kylie Minogue, Leona Lewis, Agnes Carlsson, Petra Marklund and Dolores Haze. 
As a solo artist, Quant released music in the late 1990s and early 2000s on the Swedish record label Dot.
In 2015, Quant helped British pop singer Foxes write her second studio album All I Need.
On March 16th 2021 it was announced that Jonas is the artist behind the first Swedish NFT that will be auctioned to highest bidder on March 26th 2021. https://www.dreamfng.com/
