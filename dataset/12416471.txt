Eri Kitamura (喜多村 英梨, Kitamura Eri, born August 16, 1987) is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She was previously affiliated with Early Wing, but is now a freelancer. She is known for voicing prominent roles in anime such as  and Sayaka Miki in Puella Magi Madoka Magica, Keqing in Genshin Impact, Shizuku in New Game!, Darjeeling in Girls und Panzer, Uni/Black Sister in Hyperdimension Neptunia, Ranko Honjō in My First Girlfriend Is a Gal,  Yuka Mochida in Corpse Party, Kirika Ueno in C3, and Miki Aono/Cure Berry in Fresh Pretty Cure!. In addition, she provided the voice for Vocaloid CUL-REBIRTH. She is known to be an amateur manga artist in Japan in her spare time.
