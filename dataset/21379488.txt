During the summer of 2019 he released the song "Letting Go" together with the singer KIDDO. The song reached the Swedish Top 50 Chart on Spotify as well as reaching Sverigetopplistan for a total of 13 weeks. "Letting Go" gained international attention when Kygo started to play the song during his own gigs. Hogland has now amassed over 130 million streams on Spotify, and has been IFPI certifield gold in Sweden.
