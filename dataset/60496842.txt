Grant Taylor Singer (born July 23, 1985) is an American music video and commercial director known for working with artists such as The Weeknd, Taylor Swift, Ariana Grande, Shawn Mendes, Don Toliver, Travis Scott, Camila Cabello, Sky Ferreira, Lorde, Sam Smith, Troye Sivan, Slowdive, Ariel Pink, Skrillex, Future and Zayn.
