Takuji Kawakubo (川久保 拓司, Kawakubo Takuji, born 17 December 1981, in Ōta, Tokyo, Japan) is a Japanese actor and tarento who has appeared in a number of television series, feature films, and stage productions. He is represented with Stardust Promotion. He graduated from Rikkyo University.
