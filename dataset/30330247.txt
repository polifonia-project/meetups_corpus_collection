Soledad Bengoecha de Cármena (21 March 1849 – 1893) was a Spanish composer. She was born in Madrid, and studied music with Arriola, Jesús de Monasterio and Nicolás Rodríguez Ledesma. She died in Madrid.


== Works ==
Selected works include:

Flor de los cielos, zarzuela (1894)
El gran día, zarzuela (1894)
A la fuerza ahorcan, zarzuela (1876)
Sybille, overture (1873)
Mass (1867)
Marcha triunfal
