Lova Inger Irene Drevstam (born 4 November 1992), known as Lovad, is a Swedish singer and songwriter.


== Career ==
Under the stage name, Lova, she released her debut single "Min syster sa" in 2018. In 2019, Lovad was named as one of the Stockholms Topp 5 by Sveriges Radio. She was also awarded a music and songwriting scholarship in STIM.In 2020, she released a single with Albin Johnsén titled "Vi gjorde vårt bästa". On 1 December 2020, Lovad announced that she would participate in Melodifestivalen 2021 with the song "Allting är precis likadant", co-written by her, along with Johnsén, Mattias Andréasson and Alexander Nivek. Her song, "Allting är precis likadant", failed to qualify from the semi-finals and was eliminated.
