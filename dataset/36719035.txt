Fuad Almuqtadir (Bengali: ফুয়াদ আল মুকতাদির) is a Bangladeshi-American music Record Producer, Composer, Musician and Singer-Songwriter. Fuad's style of music incorporates with fusion, folk, rock, pop and electronic dance music.
