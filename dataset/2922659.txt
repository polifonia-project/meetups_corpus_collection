Mitsuhiro Hidaka (日高 光啓, Hidaka Mitsuhiro, born December 12, 1986, in Chiba, Chiba Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese rapper, singer, actor, dancer, and a member of the J-pop group AAA. He is the rapper of the group and also writes rap lyrics for the band. He is also a member of the hip hop trio Mother Ninjas. He also debuted as a solo artist in August 2013 as Sky-Hi.
