Miu Chu (Chinese: 朱俐靜; Wade–Giles: Chu1 Li4-ching4) is a Taiwanese singer. She was the winner of the third season of the Taiwanese reality television show Super Idol.Chu performed at the Kaohsiung lantern festival in a concert on 12 February 2011. She has also performed at Legacy Taipei.
