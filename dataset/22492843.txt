Steffen Brandt  (born 14 June 1953 in Åbyhøj) is a Danish singer-songwriter and composer. Since 1981 he has been lead singer of rock group TV-2, as he also wrote both text and music to all the group's songs.In 2003 he received the Modersmål language award for his contribution to music in the Danish language.
