Péter Szilágyi (28 May 1954 – 30 May 2013) was a Hungarian music conductor and politician, member of the National Assembly (MP) for Berettyóújfalu (Hajdú-Bihar County Constituency V) between 1994 and 2002. He was a member of the Committee on Education and Science.Szilágyi died on 30 May 2013 at the age of 59.
