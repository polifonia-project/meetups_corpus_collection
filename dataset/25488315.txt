Ted Key (born Anthony Matthew Key, 1 July 1960, Hull, East Riding of Yorkshire, England) was the original bassist in The Housemartins. He was replaced in 1985 by Norman Cook.Key originally played with a local band called The Gargoyles and continues to be involved in the Hull music scene and is the front man of Ted Key and the Kingstons.Key taught mathematics at  Hull College.  He is married to a BBC Radio Humberside producer Katy Noone and has two sons.
