Matt McManamon is a Liverpool-born singer-songwriter and guitarist most known for his role as lead singer for The Dead 60s. McManamon has also played lead guitar for The Specials, and has toured and recorded with several other artists including The BibleCode Sundays.He is currently recording his first solo album, and is touring in 2019 with Ian Prowse.
