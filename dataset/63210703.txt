Chloe Moriondo (stylized in all lowercase as chloe moriondo) (born September 29, 2002) is an American singer-songwriter and YouTuber born and based in Detroit. Their style blends elements of indie pop and bedroom pop. Their YouTube channel, which they began in January 2014, sits at over 3 million subscribers and over 132 million video views. Moriondo self-released their first studio album, Rabbit Hearted., on July 31, 2018, and their first major label EP, Spirit Orb, on April 24, 2020. In August 2020, Moriondo was signed to Fueled by Ramen and released "I Want To Be With You". On May 7, 2021, Moriondo released their second studio album, Blood Bunny.


== Personal life ==
Moriondo supports the Black Lives Matter movement.  They use she/her and they/them. They currently are in a relationship with their girlfriend Samantha.
