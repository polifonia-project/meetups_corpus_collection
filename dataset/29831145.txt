Durga Jasraj (born 12 September 1966) is an Indian original content producer. She focuses on creating indigenous content in formats for multiple media, viz. Live, Television, Online, Mobile VAS, Radio, CD/DVDs. She founded "Art And Artistes (I) Pvt. Ltd.", an entertainment programming company in 1999. She subsequently co-founded the Indian Music Academy (IMA) in 2006.


== Early life ==
Jasraj was born in Mumbai to classical vocalist Pandit Jasraj and Madhura Pandit. She has an older brother, music director Shaarang Dev. Her maternal grandfather was Bollywood director V. Shantaram.


== Career ==
Jasraj started her career as a classical vocalist at age seven. Her acting debut came in Ketan Anand's Aaja Meri Jaan (1993). She acted in TV series including Chandrakanta (1994) and Mahabharat (1988). In the  Mahabharat she acted as Ulupi. She performed as a Hindustani classical singer and with her father for many years.
She became popular on Zee TV's show, Antakshari as a co-host with Annu Kapoor during the 1990s.She quit the show to start her company Art And Artistes (I) Pvt Ltd.

== Personal life ==
Durga Jasraj married at age 18 and was divorced at 21. She has a daughter.
