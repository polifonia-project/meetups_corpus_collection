Jo Jung-chi (Korean: 조정치; born August 25, 1978) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actor. He is best known for starring in the reality TV show We Got Married 4 alongside his now real-life wife, singer Choi Jung-in, in 2013.


== Personal life ==
After dating for eleven years, Jo married Choi Jung-in in 2013. They welcomed their first child, a daughter, in February 2017. Their second child, a son, was born in December 2019.
