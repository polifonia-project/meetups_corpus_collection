Özge Özpirinçci (born 1 April 1986) is a Turkish television and movie actress. She began her acting career with the TV series Cesaretin Var mı Aşka and Kavak Yelleri. Özpirinçci starred in feature films Veda and Anadolu Kartalları. Her short film The Loss won multiple awards at international film festivals. She also hosted a television program called Toplu Hayat. She also starred in Al Yazmalım, Aşk Yeniden and Kadın.
