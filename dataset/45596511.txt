Danny Fury (born in Basel) is a Swiss drummer and singer. Fury has been a member of Rogue Male, The Lords of the New Church, Kill City Dragons, Vain, The Dogs D'Amour and Sham 69.After playing in several bands and touring Switzerland, France and Germany, as well as working as a session drummer at Musicland Studios in Munich. Fury moved to London in 1984. as of 2011 Danny Formed and became the vocalist of the Tango Pirates. now also Known as "Danny Fury's Tango Pirates".
