Mie Sonozaki (園崎 未恵, Sonozaki Mie, born February 7, 1973) is a Japanese actress, voice actress and singer from Tokyo, Japan. She is best known for her role as Arle Nadja in the long-running video game series, Puyo Puyo. She is also the official Japanese dubbing roles for Hayley Atwell, Elisha Cuthbert, Kirsten Dunst and many more.
