Franco Marazzi (1926 – September 2014) was an Italian director. He directed 9 films and shows in 7 years. He is mostly known for being the director in the Eurovision Song Contest 1956.Marazzi started his career as a reporter in 1954 in Zürich. He first directed the Eurovision Song Contest 1956.
