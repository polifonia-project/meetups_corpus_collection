Eoin O'Broin (Irish pronunciation: [ˈoːnʲ oː ˈbˠɾˠɪnʲ]), better known by his stage name Noisestorm, is an Irish DJ and music producer. He is best known for his song "Crab Rave", which peaked at 14 on Billboard's Dance/Electronic Songs chart.


== Career ==
On 1 April 2018 O'Broin released his song "Crab Rave" on Monstercat as an April Fools' Day joke. The song gained popularity as an internet meme, with the song debuting at number 36 on Billboard's "Hot Dance/Electronic Songs" category. Following "Crab Rave", O'Broin released the song "Breakout" with English hip hop group Foreign Beggars. Critics praised the release, with Dancing Astronaut praising the mixture of rap with the synths and drops.
