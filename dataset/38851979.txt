M Three (Mスリー) is a Japanese all-female singing trio. It is a subunit (subgroup) of the girl group Fairies.The members are Miki, Miria and Mahiro. The names of all three start with the Latin letter M.


== Members ==
Miki Shimomura (下村実生, born October 22, 1998)
Miria Fujita (藤田みりあ, born June 15, 1998)
Mahiro Hayashida (林田真尋, born May 7, 1998)
