Karl Zaruba (December 29, 1902 – October 5, 1978 ) was an Austrian composer and conductor, founder of the Neue Meister Deutsch Kapelle (Neue Deutschmeister Band).He was the conductor of the Austrian Folk Musicians and director of the Schuhplattler Dance Band


== Discography ==
