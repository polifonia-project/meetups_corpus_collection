Ron Miles (born May 9, 1963) is an American jazz trumpeter, cornetist, and composer. He has recorded for the labels Prolific (1986), Capri (1990), and Gramavision.


== Career ==
Miles moved to Denver, Colorado from Indianapolis at the age of 11 and attended Denver East High School. He studied music at the University of Denver (1981â€“1985) and the Manhattan School of Music (1986). He is among Denver's most prominent jazz musicians. He is the co-ordinator of Jazz Studies at Metropolitan State University of Denver.
