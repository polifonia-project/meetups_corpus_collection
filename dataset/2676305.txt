Yu Yamada (山田 優, Yamada Yū, born July 5, 1984, Okinawa) is a Japanese model, actress and singer best known from her modeling work with CanCam magazine.


== Personal life ==
On March 14, 2012, Yamada married Japanese actor Shun Oguri, and is a mother of three children, as of April 2020, according to reports made by an undisclosed acquaintance.
