She was born in Legnica, Poland, and studied composition with Tadeusz Natanson at the State High School of Music in Wroclaw, where she graduated in 1976. After completing her studies, she took a teaching position at the Bielsko-Biala Music School. She married composer and accordionist Andrzej Krzanowski and often collaborated with him.


== Works ==
Selected compositions include: 

Melodies, 1975
Cantata, 1975
Passacaglia for Orchestra, 1976
Relief X for Sax quartet, 2002, dedicated to Andrzej Krzanowski
