Katarina Živković (Serbian Cyrillic: Катарина Живковић, born 2 July 1989) is a Serbian Pop-folk singer and television personality from Leskovac. She rose to prominence as a contestant on the televised singing contest Zvezde Granda in 2007. Her debut album, titled Ludo srce, was released in 2013.Živković won the third series of Serbian reality TV show Farma (2010) with 54% of public votes, receiving main cash prize of €100,000.
