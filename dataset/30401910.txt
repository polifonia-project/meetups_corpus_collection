Clara Angela Macirone (20 January 1821 â€“ 1914) was an English pianist and composer who published her music as C. A. Macirone. Born in London, she was the daughter of Italian musicians; her mother was also a pianist and her father was a singer. After completing her studies at the Royal Academy of Music she took a position teaching at the Academy. She was a pioneer in the musical education of women as a teacher and writer. She died in London in 1914.
