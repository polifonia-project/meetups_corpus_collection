Brad Wolf (born in Caryville, Tennessee) is an American country music singer. He is the great-great-grandson of Fiddlin' John Carson.Wolf recorded for Warner Bros. Records in 2003, releasing the single "Strictly Business." The song appeared on a NASCAR-themed compilation album titled Inside Traxx and was used by NBC on the TV series The Apprentice. The song spent four weeks on the Hot Country Songs charts, peaking at No. 56.
