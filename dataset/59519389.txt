Bob Schneider is a Canadian singer-lyricist, best known for his children's music. He is best known as the lead singer of Bob Schneider and the Rainbow Kids. Schenider was a nominee for the Juno Award for Children's Album of the Year in 1992 for Listen to the Children. The following year, he won this Juno Award for When You Dream a Dream.
