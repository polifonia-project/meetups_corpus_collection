Park Soo-jin (Korean: 박수진; born December 4, 1995) is a South Korean singer. She joined Brave Entertainment in 2014 while she was a semi-finalist on the MBC audition program The Great Birth Season 3. She also appeared on Immortal Song: Singing the Legend in 2014.


== Music career ==


=== 2013–present: Solo debut ===
Park Soo-jin officially debuted on March 14, 2013 with her first single "My Story". On December 11, 2014 she came out with her second single "Fallin" with a music video released the same day.
