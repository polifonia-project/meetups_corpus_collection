Jason Pierce is a Canadian drummer, currently the drummer of the Canadian band Our Lady Peace since 2016.


== Career ==
During the 2000s, Pierce was a member of the Canadian band The Weekend.In 2012, Pierce performed drums on the Evans Blue album Graveyard of Empires. He was also the drummer for Treble Charger for a time.In 2014, while in search of a replacement drummer, Our Lady Peace enlisted Pierce to perform during live shows. In 2016, he officially joined the band as their full-time drummer.He has also toured with Carly Rae Jepsen, Justin Bieber, Paramore, Dallas Green, and Chantal Kreviazuk, among others.
