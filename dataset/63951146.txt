Oswald Antoine Marie d'Andréa is a French pianist and composer of music for film, television and radio. He was born on August 9, 1934 in Tunis.His recorded output varied from pop in the early '60s to jazz and movie soundtracks, one of which—La Vie et Rien d'Autre—won the 1990 César for Best Music.
