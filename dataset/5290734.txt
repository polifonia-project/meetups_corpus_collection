Željko "Žeki" Šašić (Serbian Cyrillic: Жељко Шашић; born 31 October 1969) is a Serbian turbo-folk singer. In late 2013, he competed in the first season of the Serbian version of Your Face Sounds Familiar. After the 12 weeks, he claimed 7th place, but was a public favourite with a wide spectre of enjoyed performances as Aca Lukas, Bijelo Dugme, Riblja Čorba, Snežana Babić Sneki and many more.
