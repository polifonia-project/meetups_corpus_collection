Angela Ken Tavares Rojas (born May 16, 2002), better known as Angela Ken, is a Filipina actress, singer, songwriter, dancer and influencer. She gained acclaim on social networking service TikTok. After releasing her single "Ako Naman Muna", she signed with Star Magic, a talent agency under ABS-CBN Corporation. Angela is also a member of the Squad Plus. In 2022, Angela is set to make her acting debut in the iQIYI series titled Lyric and Beat.
