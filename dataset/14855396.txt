Anatoly Avgustovich Gunitsky (Russian: Анато́лий А́вгустович Гуни́цкий, IPA: [ɐnɐˈtolʲɪj ˈavɡʊstəvʲɪdʑ ɡʊˈnʲitskʲɪj] (listen); born September 30, 1953), also known as George and Old Rocker (Старый рокер), is a Russian writer, journalist, and poet. He is one of the founders of Aquarium.
Since the foundation of Aquarium in 1972 he was the drummer of group and the author of many lyrics. In 1975 he left the group and participated in absurd theatre actions. In that period he wrote "Do samikh visot", "Smert bezbiletnika" and other plays.
