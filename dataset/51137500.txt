Kenneth Geoffrey Oudejans (born Amsterdam, Netherlands (1987-01-27)27 January 1987), better known by his stage name Kenneth G, is a Dutch DJ and record producer.He became known in 2013 with his releases on the Dutch label Hysteria Records before joining Revealed Recordings the following year.
