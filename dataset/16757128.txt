Matthew Moses Brunson (born November 19, 1981) is an American musician who is the lead guitarist for sludge metal band Crowbar and bassist for Kingdom of Sorrow.
Brunson is from Metairie in Jefferson Parish, Louisiana and formerly played guitar in Shedding Old Skin. He attended East Jefferson High School in Metairie.
