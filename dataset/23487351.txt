Bit_Meddler is the alias of experimental musician Matt Anderson. His musical compositions incorporate glitch and mashup styles.
He worked for the design agency Soft Gold U.S.A. and performed at Pukkelpop 2001 in Belgium.A music video of his track "Cut" by Till Heim earned an honorable mention at the 2005 International Short Film Festival Oberhausen.
