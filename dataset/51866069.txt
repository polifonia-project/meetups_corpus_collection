Hiroki Maekawa (前川 紘毅, Maekawa Hiroki, born 9 November 1985, in Setagaya, Tokyo, Japan), known professionally as Hiroki (紘毅), is a Japanese singer-songwriter. He dropped out from Shobi University. He is represented by Avex Vanguard.
