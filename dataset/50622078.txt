Stephen Michael Swartz (born December 13, 1991), known professionally as Stephen, is an American electronic music artist, singer-songwriter, and producer. He is known for his song "Crossfire" and his cover of Adele's song "Hello". He collaborated with Pell and Caleborate in the song "In the Morning".
Stephen's debut album, Sincerely, was released on May 10, 2016. His sophomore album, Akrasia, was released on August 21, 2020.
