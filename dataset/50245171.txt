Hibiku Yamamura (山村 響, Yamamura Hibiku, born February 10, 1988) is a Japanese voice actress and singer. As a solo singer, she uses the name hibiku.  She is affiliated with Haikyō.On July 7, 2021, it was announced Yamamura had tested positive for COVID-19. She recovered on July 14, 2021.
