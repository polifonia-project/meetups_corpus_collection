Terunosuke Takezai (竹財 輝之助, Takezai Terunosuke, born April 7, 1980 in Kumamoto Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese actor. He is sometimes referred to as a promotion video actor due to his numerous appearances in promotion videos.
