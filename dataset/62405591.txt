Long Beard is the stage name of New Brunswick, New Jersey musician Leslie Bear, born 23 October 1990.


== History ==
Bear released her first EP, Holy Crow, in 2014. Bear released her first album in 2015 on Team Love Records titled Sleepwalker.In 2019, Bear released her second full-length album and first on Double Double Whammy titled Means to Me.


== Influences ==
Bear listened to Faye Wong growing up.
