Mateusz Maurycy Śmierzchalski (born 18 July 1982, Gdynia, Poland), stage name Havoc, is a Polish heavy metal musician. He is best known for being the former rhythm guitarist for Behemoth. Since 1999, he is lead guitarist of the sludge-doom metal band Blindead.Mateusz Śmierzchalski is endorsed by Mayones.
