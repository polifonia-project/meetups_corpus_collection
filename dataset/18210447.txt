Marvin Scandrick, better known as Slim, is an American contemporary R&B singer best known as the lead singer/frontman for the group 112. His 2008 debut album Love's Crazy peaked at #32 on the Billboard 200. It included the hit singles "So Fly", which reached #49 on the Billboard Hot 100. and "Good Lovin", which reached #39 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart.
