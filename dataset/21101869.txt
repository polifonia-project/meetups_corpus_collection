Yukio Yamagata (山形 ユキオ, Yamagata Yukio, born March 11, 1957) is a Fukuoka-born singer, actor, and voice actor. He is probably best known for singing the openings to Hyakujuu Sentai Gaoranger, Baxinger, and the final ED to Braiger. He has starred in several stage productions. In television he had a guest-star role in Gaoranger episode 6 and the role of Shishioh in Shuriken Sentai Ninninger. Yamagata performed the role of Kuro Bara Danshaku on the drama cd Cherry princess I. 
He is a close friend of fellow anison singer Isao Taira.
