Slobodan Veljković (Serbian Cyrillic: Слободан Вељковић, pronounced [slobǒdan ʋěːʎkoʋitɕ]; born 11 April 1985), known professionally as Coby (Serbian Cyrillic: Цоби, pronounced [tsǒbi]), is a Serbian rapper, songwriter and record producer. As the executive producer of Bassivity Digital he received recognition with his production work for the popular Serbian artists such as Elitni Odredi, Rasta, Ana Nikolić, Nikolija and Senidah.
