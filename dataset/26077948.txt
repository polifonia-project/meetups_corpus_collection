Terry Gregory (born Teresa Gregory Burdine; April 30, 1956 in Washington, D.C.) is an American country music singer-songwriter. Gregory's debut album, Just Like Me, was released in 1981 by Handshake Records. Its first single, the title track, reached the Top 20 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles chart.Dave Mulholland of the Ottawa Citizen gave the album a mixed review, praising Gregory's "light, lilting voice" but criticizing the "mundane lyrics".
