Hello Pro Kenshusei (ハロプロ研修生, Haro Puro Kenshūsei, lit. "Hello Pro Trainees") (formerly known as Hello Pro Egg (ハロプロエッグ) until January 2012) is a pre-debut training group established by Japanese entertainment agency Up-Front Group within Hello! Project in 2004.
