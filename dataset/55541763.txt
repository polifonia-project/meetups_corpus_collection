Kaori Sadohara (佐土原 かおり, Sadohara Kaori, born January 18, 1988) is a Japanese voice actress and singer. Her most known role is Chocolat in Noucome. She is affiliated with the agency Object. She married fellow voice actor Yoshihisa Kawahara in 2017.
