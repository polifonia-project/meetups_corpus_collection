Helen Vogt (born 14 May 1982) is a German contralto singer-songwriter. She is the founder of the trip hop band Lighthouse in Darkness, and she was the vocalist of gothic metal band Flowing Tears from 2003 to 2014.She's married to David Vogt, guitarist and bassist of Powerwolf.She started in 1999 as a vocalist of the band Sweet Infernal Noise.
