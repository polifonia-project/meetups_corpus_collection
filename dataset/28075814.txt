Salil Bhatt is an Indian slide guitar player. He is the son of the fellow slide player and Grammy Award winner, Padmashree Vishwa Mohan Bhatt.His latest album, Slide to Freedom 2 - Make a Better World, was nominated for a Canadian Juno Award.
