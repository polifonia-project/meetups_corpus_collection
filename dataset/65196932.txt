Aleksandar "Saša" Antić (pronounced [aleksǎːndar sâʃa  ǎːntitɕ]; born 15 October 1973) is a Croatian rapper, singer-songwriter and producer. He is widely known for his work as the founding member, primary lyricist and vocalist for the cult Split-based rap group The Beat Fleet, but also recorded successfully as a solo artist.
