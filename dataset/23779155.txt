Henry (or Henri) Wyn (1910 - 1989) was a Belgian composer and pianist.Wyn composed the musical scores for at least two Belgian films and also recorded as a pianist with a group called "Henri Wyn et ses Rythmes".Wyn's compositions were at least partly published by the music publishing company ""L’art belge", owned by composer Léon Frings.
