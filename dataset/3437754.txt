Allan John Atkins (born 14 October 1947) is an English heavy metal vocalist, best known for being the original lead vocalist and founder for Judas Priest.


== Biography ==
With a wife and young daughter to support, Atkins was forced "to get a 9-to-5 job" in May 1973.As of 2012, Atkins fronts the band Atkins/May Project, which also features guitarist Paul May. A press release dated 21 May 2011 stated that Atkins would be contributing session vocals for the heavy metal opera project, Lyraka. In 2013, Atkins recorded a solo album of his favorite songs written between his days in Judas Priest and his Holy Rage days.
