Nusrat Hussain (Urdu:نصرت حسین), is a Pakistani musician, airline pilot, guitarist, and keyboardist. He played guitar for the Vital Signs and keyboard for Junoon in the mid 1990s. He soon left both bands opting for a career in PIA as an airline pilot in airline industry.After his departure from Junoon, Nusrat released a solo album Amrit, which featured popular tracks such as Jo Chaho Tum and Teri Awaz. He made a comeback in 2015 with his album Kaho, which was a collaboration between him and music producer Sarmad Ghafoor.Nusrat Hussain currently captains Boeing 777 aircraft for Qatar Airways.
