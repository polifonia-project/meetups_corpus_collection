Gordana "Goca" Božinovska (Serbian Cyrillic: Гордана "Гоца" Божиновска; born 16 January 1965) is a Serbian pop-folk singer. Born in the village of Samaila near Kraljevo, she started her career by performing in kafanas dodging Ibar Highway alongside Vesna Zmijanac. Božinovska eventually rose to prominence after being discovered by singer Šaban Šaulić.
In 2013, she was a contestant on the reality show Farma, finishing in fourth place.Božinovska was married to kick-boxer and alleged leader of Surčin Clan, Zoran Šijan, who was assassinated in 1999 under unresolved circumstances. She has three children.
