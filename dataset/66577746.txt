Bilal Göregen (born September 4, 1988), is a Turkish street musician and drummer. He is blind and has become famous via his rendition of Ievan polkka, wherein a Twitter user superimposed a vibing cat on his video.  YouTube has shared his performing video on Instagram.


== Life and career ==
Born and raised in Hatay, Turkey. Bilal Göregen has performed in O Ses Türkiye. He earned fame by Sevdiğim kız bana abi deyince. He had a video with the mayor of Istanbul Ekrem İmamoğlu. He is of Kurdish origin from Bitlis.
