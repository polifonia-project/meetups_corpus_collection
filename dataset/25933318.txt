Ephram Lopez (born January 25, 1969), know professionally as DJ Enuff is a DJ and radio personality from the United States. He mixes on-air for New York's Hip Hop radio station HOT 97 on weekdays from 4pm to 6pm. He is best known for being The Notorious B.I.G's official road DJ and also helped break acts like 50 Cent, Kanye West, and Future. In 2009 Enuff was able to reprise his role as B.I.G's DJ in the film Notorious. Lopez was born and grew up in Brooklyn.
