Chinatsu Wakatsuki (若槻 千夏, Wakatsuki Chinatsu, born 28 May 1984) is a Japanese variety tarento and former gravure idol. Her real name was Chiharu Kurihara (栗原 千春, Kurihara Chiharu) before she got married. She is represented with Platinum Production.
