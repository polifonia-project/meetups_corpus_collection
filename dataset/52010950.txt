Eiko Kano (狩野 英孝, Kano Eikō, born 22 February 1982, in Kurikoma (now Kurihara), Kurihara District, Miyagi, Japan) is a Japanese comedian, singer, YouTuber, and kannushi. He is nicknamed Eiko-chan (英孝ちゃん, Eikō-chan) and Kati (カティ). As a lyricist and composer of 50TA, he is nicknamed Shinya Sakurada (桜田山神邪, Sakurada Shin'ya). He graduated from the Japan Institute of the Moving Image. He completed his kannushi training in Kokugakuin University. He was a former kyūdōka at the National Sports Festival of Japan.
