Daryl McPherson, known as DJ Durel, is an American record producer, recording engineer, and DJ from Cleveland, Ohio. He is the official DJ of Atlanta rap group, Migos.


== Career ==
In late 2017, Control the Streets, Vol. 1, a compilation album released by Quality Control Music, featured six tracks produced by DJ Durel – more than any other producer features on the 30-track album.In 2018, Migos released Culture II, crediting DJ Durel and Quavo as executive producers of the album. DJ Durel is listed as a producer on 12 of the 24 songs on the album and as a recording engineer for the entire album.
Durel claims to be the fourth member of Migos in his Instagram bio, despite the group being a self-acknowledged trio.
