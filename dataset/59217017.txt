Kadiatou Claudine Holm Keita, (born 6 September 2001) is a Swedish singer. She grew up in Lidingö.She was one of the finalists in Idol 2018 which is broadcast on TV4. She competed against Sebastian Walldén in Globen on 7 December and placed second.Kadiatou participated in Melodifestivalen 2021 with her song "One Touch", but failed to qualify for the final after placing sixth in the first semi-final.
