Jung Yoo-jin (born February 19, 1989), also known as Eugene Jung, is a South Korean model and actress. She started her career as a model at YG Entertainment.She is known for her roles in Korean dramas, such as Heard It Through the Grapevine (2015), Moorim School and W.
In February 2018, Jung signed with new management agency FNC Entertainment.
