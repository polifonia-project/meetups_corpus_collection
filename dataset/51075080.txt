Christopher Knight (born Hertfordshire, England), better known by his stage name Kryder, is an English DJ and record producer.He is best known for his record "K2" and original track "Aphrodite" released on Axtone Records and his remix of Kölsch's "All That Matters" featuring Troels Abrahamsen both of which reached No. 1 on the Beatport Top 100 chart. He is also the owner of Kryteria Records.
Many of his other singles also experienced great success: "Treasured Soul" and "Jericho" figured many weeks in the top 100.
