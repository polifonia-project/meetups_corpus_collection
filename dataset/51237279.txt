Maey Bautista, also known as Maey B, is a Filipino journalist, actress and comedian, alongside partner, Betong Sumaya, She is best known in Day Off, May Tamang Balita, AHA!, Judith in I Heart Davao and DJ Mema in Destined to be Yours.
