Peter Froggatt (born 29 December 1934, Glossop, Derbyshire) is a British talent agent. He attended Hyde Grammar School before training at The Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London.
He founded one of Great Britain's top theatrical agencies, Plant & Froggatt in 1965. It became Markham & Froggatt, and later  Markham Froggatt and Irwin, and is still in the top ten of talent agents. He is now retired in the Cotswolds.
