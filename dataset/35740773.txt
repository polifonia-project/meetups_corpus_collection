Song Ha-yoon (born Kim Mi-sun on December 2, 1986) is a South Korean actress. She debuted as  Kim Byul (Korean: 김별) but changed her stage name to Song Ha-yoon in 2012. She is best known for her role in the drama Fight for My Way (2017).
