Ian Kirkham (born 9 March 1963, Liverpool, England) is an English saxophonist, best known for his playing with Simply Red from 1986 until the band retired in 2010. He reprised his role in the band when it reformed in 2015, and played on their 2019 album, Blue Eyed Soul. Kirkham grew up in Preston. He started learning piano at five, and saxophone at eleven.
