Jason Hsu (born 27 July 1985) is a Taiwanese singer, actor and ice hockey player. He was a member of the Taiwanese boyband 5566.


== Life and career ==
Hsu's father is a businessman. He has an elder sister.Hsu is a keen ice hockey player, and has played for the Chinese Taipei national team since 2017.
