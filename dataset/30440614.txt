Carl Melles (born Melles Károly; 15 July 1926 – 25 April 2004) was an Austrian orchestral conductor of Hungarian descent.


== Personal life ==
He married Hungarian noblewoman Judith von Rohonczy (1929–2001) and is the father of actress Sunnyi Melles, who is married to Prince Peter zu Sayn-Wittgenstein-Sayn.


== Work ==
He conducted Wagner's Tannhauser at the 1966 Bayreuth Festival.
