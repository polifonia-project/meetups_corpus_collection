Andrew Speight  is an Australian-born jazz saxophonist based in the United States. His band, the Andrew Speight Quartet, won the 1999 ARIA Award for Best Jazz Album with their self titled album.Speight had previously fronted the jazz quintet Now's the Time.

=== ARIA Music Awards ===
