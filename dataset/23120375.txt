Justine Pelmelay (born 24 September 1958, Leiden) is a singer who represented the Netherlands in the Eurovision Song Contest in 1989. Her entry, "Blijf zoals je bent" (Stay the way you are) was placed fifteenth out of the 22 participating songs.In 1996 she song duet with the song Perhaps Love with the American singer-songwriter John Denver.
Pelmelay made another attempt at Eurovision in 2005 with the song "What you see is what you get" but it did not qualify for the Dutch national final.
Pelmelay was in the Costa Concordia when it was involved in the disaster which happened on 14 January 2012.
