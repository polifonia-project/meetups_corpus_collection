Larry Herrington, known by his stage name Carmouflage Rose, is an Australian musician, singer, songwriter and rapper. He has played at Australian festivals and events including: Spilt Milk and Groovin The Moo. Larry signed a deal with Sony Music Australia in 2018. His song "Late Nights" was streamed 8.5 million times and was one of the top 30 songs played Triple J in 2017, and was the Gold in 2018.On 19 March 2021, Carmouflage Rose released the double-A sided single "Tipsy"/"Powerplay".
