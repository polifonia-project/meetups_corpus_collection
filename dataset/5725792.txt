Dragan Kojić (Serbo-Croatian Cyrillic: Драган Којић; born 7 October 1956), better known as Keba (Serbo-Croatian Cyrillic: Кеба), is a Serbian and former Yugoslavian singer.
Debuted in 1976, he has a career spanning more than four decades and remains one of the most acclaimed Serbian folk singers.Kojić also appeared in the second season of the Serbian version of Your Face Sounds Familiar in 2014.He is married and has two children, former singer Nataša Kojić and former footballer Igor Kojić.
