Controller 7 (born Tommy McMahon) is an underground hip hop producer from San Jose, California.


== History ==
Controller 7 released the debut album Left Handed Straw in 2000. It was reissued on 6months in 2001.He has produced and remixed tracks for Anticon artists such as Sole, Sage Francis and Themselves.The Bumps mixtape was released in 2005.
