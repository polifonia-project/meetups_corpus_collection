Ljubomir Aličić (Serbian Cyrillic: Љубомир Аличић; born 2 November 1955), known by his stage name Ljuba Aličić (Serbian Cyrillic: Љуба Аличић), is a Serbian and former FPR-Yugoslavian folk singer.
His career has been lasting more than five decades.His biggest hit is the 2003 single-song "Ciganin sam, al’ najlepši".
