Sricharan Pakala is an Indian music composer who works in the Telugu film industry. Sricharan entered the film industry as a music director with the Telugu film KISS in 2013. He went on to become popular for his work as a music director. He made his acting debut with Krishna and His Leela (2020) for which he also composed music.


== Personal life and career ==
He was born in Visakhapatnam into a Telugu-speaking family.
