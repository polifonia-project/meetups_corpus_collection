L. Beth Cohen (born 1970 or 1971 on Long Island, New York) is an American rock musician and songwriter. She has toured with Barry Gibb and the Bee Gees, Jon Secada and Chayanne. She has also appeared on stage with Julio Iglesias, Kelly Clarkson and Isaac Hayes. She has done session work for Jon Secada, Barbara Streisand, Boston, Pink, Teddy Geiger, Alejandro Sans and Paulina Rubio. In 2015 she joined Boston.
