Heikki Liimatainen, known professionally as Heikki L,  is a house music producer, DJ and remixer from Finland.


== Biography ==
Heikki Liimatainen has released music under different aliases like Heikki L, Supermodels From Paris, Syndicate of Beats, Dallas Superstars, Modulation, Moovon, Spectro, Hemohes and Bostik. Under his current alias, Heikki L, he has produced many big house tracks and also remixed many artists.
