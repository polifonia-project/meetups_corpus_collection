Ivan Yastrebov (born 16 November 2002) is a Turkish - Russian singer.He started his career at the Ukrainian State Theatre. He took part in the show called Folk Dance broadcast on 1+1 TV.  After , he came 2nd in the Junior Eurovision Ukraine national selections.  And then he competed with the song "Argo" in the 4th season of The Voice Kids Russia. İn 2016, he joined The Voice Kids Ukraine and became one of the finalists of the competition.And  in 2018, he became the winner of "Иван Синяя птица" (song contest in Russia). In 2021, he was transferred for Universal Music - Spinnup record label.
