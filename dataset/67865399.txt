Jesse Ray Ernster is a Canadian-born American mixing engineer, audio engineer, and record producer. He has worked with artists including Kanye West, Burna Boy, and Goody Grace.Ernster has earned two Grammy Awards for his contributions to the albums Jesus Is King by Kanye West and Twice as Tall by Burna Boy.
