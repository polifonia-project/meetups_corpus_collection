Sadako Ruth Pointer Johnson (born March 24, 1984), known professionally as Sadako Pointer, is an American singer.


== Biography ==
Born in 1984, Sadako Pointer is the granddaughter of Ruth Pointer of the Pointer Sisters. She grew up in New York City and Los Angeles, California.  Sadako frequently tours with The Pointer Sisters, with Ruth and Great-Aunt Anita Pointer, and appeared in the 2014 film Proxy.
