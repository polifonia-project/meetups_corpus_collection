Bertil H. van Boer (born October 2, 1952) is an American musicologist, composer, conductor and violist.


== Life and career ==
Bertil Hermann van Boer studied at the University of California, Berkeley. He received his Master of Arts at the University of Oregon and his PhD in Music History from Uppsala University.Van Boer is Professor of Music at Western Washington University.
