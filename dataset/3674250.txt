Yumi Kawai (河合由美, Kawai Yumi, born June 8, 1966 in Soka, Saitama Prefecture, Japan), better known by the stage name Yumi Morio (森尾由美, Morio Yumi), is a Japanese actress and singer employed by the talent management firm Stardust Promotion.
