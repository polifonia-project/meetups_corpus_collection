Anders Iwers (born 6 October 1972) is a Swedish heavy metal musician and bassist of the bands Tiamat and Avatarium. He is a former member of the band Dark Tranquillity. He is the older brother of former In Flames bassist Peter Iwers.


== Biography ==
Anders is the former guitarist of bands Ceremonial Oath (also being the founder), Cemetary, Lacuna Coil, as well as a co-founder and former session musician for In Flames. He was also the bassist of Mercury Tide and Avatarium. Anders did photography on the albums A Moonclad Reflection and Yesterworlds by Dark Tranquillity.
