Udoma Peter Kelvin Amba popularly known by his trademark It's Kel-P Vibes is a Nigerian record producer, singer and songwriter. He is best known for producing and co-writing the Grammy nominated world album African Giant in 2019. He is currently signed to Aristokrat Records. Kel-P was credited by OkayAfrica, as one of the key figure who have helped take Afrobeats to new heights.


== Early life ==
Udoma Peter Kelvin Amba was born and raised in Lagos State.
