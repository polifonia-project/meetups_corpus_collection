Jennifer Rachael Zigrino (born January 8, 1987) is an American comedian, singer and actress. Zigrino had supporting roles in the comedy films Fifty Shades of Black (2016) and Bad Santa 2 (2016).Zigrino was a guest on @midnight, Conan and Comedy Central Stand-Up Presents in 2017, and Live from Here in 2018.Zigrino released her debut album named 'JZ's New Album' in March 2017.
