Emilia Bashur (Bulgarian: Емилия Башур, née Valeva), known by her stage name Emilia, is a Bulgarian pop folk singer. She has released eight studio albums to date.


== Early life ==
Emilia was born in Galabovo on 21 March 1982. She has one older sister, Daniela. Emilia became interested in music at an early age and began singing in the local choir. She began learning traditional Bulgarian music with Jivka Dimitrova and Dimitar Kolev. Several years later, she signed a contract with the Bulgarian record label Payner.In 1999, she recorded her first song- "Its over with you" which became popular in the pop-folk culture. Her debut album Veselo Momiche (Happy Girl) was successful.She has a son, Ivan and a daughter, Mira.
