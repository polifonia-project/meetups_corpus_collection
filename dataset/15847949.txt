Adam Kury (born July 7, 1969) is an American bassist and vocalist. He is best known as the bassist for the American alternative rock band Candlebox. Based in Los Angeles, California, he has been the bassist for Candlebox since the band reunited in 2008.Kury has also performed with notable acts such as Legs Diamond, Jagger Cook and several others. He also performed music for the movie, Gone Fishin'. He currently works in numerous projects/bands including The Kings Royal, Dave Giles, 7th Sun, and Whipped Cream.
