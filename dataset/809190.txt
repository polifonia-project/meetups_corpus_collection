Kana Ueda (植田 佳奈, Ueda Kana, born June 9, 1980) is a Japanese voice actress and singer represented by I'm Enterprise. She is a graduate of Kobe College's department of General Culture.
She is best known as the voices of Yumi Fukuzawa in Maria-sama ga Miteru, Rin Tōhsaka in Fate/stay night, Hayate Yagami in Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha A's and Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha Strikers, and Rachel Alucard in BlazBlue. Ueda was raised in Osaka Prefecture and is known for her strong Kansai accent in some of her roles.
