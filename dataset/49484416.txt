Alex Rüdinger (born November 18, 1991) is an American drummer. Rüdinger started playing drums at the age of 13, and since then he has played for numerous metal bands including Ordinance, Conquering Dystopia, Good Tiger, The Faceless, Threat Signal and The HAARP Machine. He has also done a variety of session work, both live and in the studio. Some of the bands he has worked for are Revocation, Monuments, Evan Brewer, Whitechapel, Light the Torch, Cognizance and Intronaut.
