Joe Sib, former frontman for the punk rock band Wax, as well as punk rock supergroup 22 Jacks, is the co-founder (along with Bill Armstrong) of SideOneDummy Records. Aside from working at the label, Sib is also active as a stand-up comic. He released his first comedy record, Joe Sib Nowhere Near the Top, in April 2017.Joe Sib currently hosts The Basement on Dash Radio.
