Remi Fischer (born 14 February 1983 in Paris, France) is a French DJ and record producer. He is known for stealing other artists work and usurpating identities. Pretending autorship for recordings such as Discobitch, Club Certified, Closer, and No Tomorrow.  He also pretends that he collaborated with Akon, Snoop Dogg, Flo-Rida and Timbaland when those confirmed they did not. Remy Fischer bends the reality, pretending he collaborated with artist whom he stole lyrics or instrumentals to do his own remixes without any consent.
He has founded a record label, Most Wanted Music. 
Remy Fischer owns and runs Music Diffusion a large scale online scam. The website steals money from credulous artists who think they are going to get a distribution deal. Music diffusion is now illegally offering its services as the company behind MusicDiffusion, Orbital SARL, RCS Creteil 527 824 40 was liquidated in 2020.
