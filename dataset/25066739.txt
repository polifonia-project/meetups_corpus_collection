Klaus Ignatzek is a German jazz pianist, composer and band leader who has recorded as leader for Nagel-Heyer Records, Red Records, Timeless Records, and Candid Records.Ignatzek was born November 4,1954 in Wilhelmshaven, Germany. As jazz pianist he has been part of the formations Crossing, Jazzwheel, Klaus Ignatzek / Florian Poser Duo, Klaus Ignatzek / Martin Wind Duo, Klaus Ignatzek Group, Klaus Ignatzek Quartet, Klaus Ignatzek Quintet, Klaus Ignatzek Trio, The Klaus Ignatzek Jazztet, Voss Ignatzek Duo.
