Jérémy Amelin (born July 1, 1986) is a French recording artist and entertainer. Best known as the finalist of French music TV show Star Academy 5.


== Biography ==
Amelin was born on July 1, 1986 in Montargis, France and raised in Montcresson, France.
He was the finalist of French music TV show Star Academy 5 — broadcast on TV channel TF1.Amelin released five singles, completed a nationwide tour, and created his own clothing line called Elektrode.He appeared as Captain Phoebus in the 10-year anniversary tour of musical Notre Dame de Paris in Seoul, South Korea..
Amelin also performed in the cabaret musical Flamboyant at the Royal Palace in Kirrwiller, France.
