Richard Timothy Sult, known as Tim Sult, is the guitarist for hard rock band Clutch. He is also the guitarist for an instrumental side project, The Bakerton Group,  and an occasional member of the reggae rock / stoner rock band Lionize, as well as the band Deep Swell. Sult has remained the guitarist for Clutch since the group started in 1990.
