Adam Nayyar (1948, in Bahawalpur â€“ July 29, 2008 in Islamabad) was a Pakistani anthropologist, author and ethnomusicologist. He served the Lok Virsa for twenty years as a Director Research. He was the acting Executive Director of Pakistan National Council of the Arts (PNCA) when he died.


== Early life ==
Nayyar was born in 1948 in Bahawalpur. He studied Cultural anthropology at the University of Heidelberg, Germany.


== Career ==
He was also the faculty of the Quaid-e-Azam University, Islamabad.


== Death ==
Nayyar died on July 29, 2008 in Islamabad due to cancer of lymph nodes.
