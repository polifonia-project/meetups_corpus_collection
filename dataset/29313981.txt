Her compositions include two children's operas, a concerto for piano and orchestra, and a Piano Quintet.  She has published an article which is featured in the book Sergei Rachmaninoff: a bio-bibliography on Rachmaninoff's visit to Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia. Eksanishvili died in January 2003 at the age of 83.
