Lionel C. Martin is a music video director, film director and VJ from Queens, New York. While he is known primarily as a music video director, he has also directed the films Def Jam's How to Be a Player and Longshot.Martin also co-created and co-hosts the music video program Video Music Box with Ralph McDaniels.
