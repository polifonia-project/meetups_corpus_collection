Sultan Karimov (Kazakh: Сұлтан Кәрімов, Sultan Kárimov), (born 13 September 1993) better known by his stage name Farleon, is a Kazakh electronic musician, record producer, remixer and DJ.In the second half of 2012 he entered the scene with his EP "Millenium", which entered top five releases of the week on Beatport. In 2016, his single "Tell Me" caught an eye of Don Diablo, who subsequently signed his collaboration with Dropgun "Fever" to Hexagon.
