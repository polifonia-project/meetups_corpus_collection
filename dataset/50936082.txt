Santos Montano is a drummer from Santa Fe, New Mexico. He is known for playing on the first album of post-metal band Zozobra and for being a founding member and the drummer of post-metal band Old Man Gloom. In addition to his music career, he is working as part of an art department in the movie industry. He has worked for both series productions (e.g. Marvel's Daredevil, released in 2015) as well as for movie productions (e.g. Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb). He has also written one op-ed for Pitchfork.
