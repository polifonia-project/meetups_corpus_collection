Mariya Nishiuchi (西内 まりや, Nishiuchi Mariya, born December 24, 1993 in Fukuoka Prefecture) is a Japanese model, actress, and singer-songwriter who is affiliated with Rising Production. She is the younger sister of model, actress, and BeForU member Hiromi Nishiuchi.
