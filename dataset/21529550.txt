Ai Haruna (はるな 愛, Haruna Ai, born 21 July 1972)  is a Japanese transgender TV personality and singer.In October 2009, Haruna won the "Miss International Queen 2009" transgender beauty pageant held in Pattaya, Thailand, becoming the first Japanese contestant to win the title.
