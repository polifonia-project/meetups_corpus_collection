Sam de Jong (born 20 March 1989), is a record producer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist from Auckland, New Zealand based in Los Angeles, California.


== Career ==
Making his start in the music industry at 16 years old as a touring drummer, Sam is best known for his work as a producer and songwriter for artists including; Rudimental, Alec Benjamin, Lennon Stella, Gracie Abrams, Amy Shark, Marina, Little Mix, Pale Waves, Noah Kahan, Maisie Peters, Anson Seabra, Thirty Seconds to Mars, Gary Clark Jr & Muse.Sam is published by Sony Music Publishing, and is based in Los Angeles.
