Stacey Glick is a former American child actress who was born December 29, 1971, in Brooklyn, New York.
Her most prominent roles came in the late-1980s in the films Brighton Beach Memoirs and Three O'Clock High. The latter was also her last film role.
Stacey Glick now works as a literary agent.
