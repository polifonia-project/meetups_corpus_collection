Don Sickler (January 6, 1944) is an American jazz trumpeter, arranger and producer.In the 1980s, he set up a tribute band called Dameronia with Philly Joe Jones, to play the music of Tadd Dameron.
He also worked extensively with Joe Henderson, a collaboration leading to four Grammy awards.
