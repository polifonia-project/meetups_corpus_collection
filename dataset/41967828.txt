Tomasz Halicki, (born December 18, 1976), is a Polish musician and singer. Halicki has played with such bands as Stygmat, Via Mistica, Diseased, and Dead Infection. He currently plays in Abused Majesty, Hermh, Evil Machine, and Vader.Hal is endorsed by Warwick Bass Guitars & Amps
