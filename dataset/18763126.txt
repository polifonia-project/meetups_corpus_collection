Theodore (Ted) G. Brown, born December 1, 1927 in Rochester, New York, is a cool jazz tenor saxophonist.Brown has worked with Warne Marsh and Ronnie Ball, and recorded with Lennie Tristano, Art Pepper, Hod O'Brien and Lee Konitz, as well as heading his own groups.
