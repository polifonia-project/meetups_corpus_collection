Joseph J. Bouchard (; born 9 November 1948) is an American guitarist and bassist. He was the bassist for Blue Öyster Cult during their most successful period. He is also the younger brother of original Blue Öyster Cult drummer Albert Bouchard.


== Biography ==
Bouchard was born in Watertown, New York.
