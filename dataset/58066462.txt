Ana Mena Rojas (born 25 February 1997) is a Spanish singer and actress. She began her career in 2009 in the TV series Marisol, la película (2009). In 2010, she participated in Disney's musical reality show My Camp Rock 2, ending up as the winner.Her musical career began in 2016, when she released her debut single "No Soy Como Tú Crees". Mena has collaborated with many well known artists since, including Becky G, CNCO and Lali. In 2018, she released her debut studio album, Index.
