Katharina Elisabeth Meyer née Tuxen (b. 19 July 1859, d. 4 July 1927) was a Danish composer. She was born in Svendborg, Denmark, sister of singer Marie English. She studied music with her mother and possibly composers Hans Jørgen Malling and Ludwig Birkedal Barford. She married and had four children, and died in Copenhagen.


== Works ==
Meyer wrote about sixty songs and a cantata (lost). Selected works include:

The Lark
Berceuse for violin and pianoforte
Naar Duggen falder for pianoforte
