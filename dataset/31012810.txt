Choi Seung Hyun, also known as Shawn Choi, is a South Korean film score composer. He has composed music for movies such as Oldboy, The Classic, Windstruck, My Girlfriend Is an Agent, and many more. His compositions were huge hits and are well known to movie fans around the world. He has also composed music for video games and television. Choi is the founder of PSALM Music Production and is focusing on contemporary Christian music.
