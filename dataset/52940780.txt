Cha Yoon-ji (Korean: 차윤지; born December 2, 1996), better known by her stage name I (아이), is a South Korean singer and dancer formally under WM Entertainment. She officially debuted on January 12, 2017 with her debut EP, I Dream. She joined The Unit: Idol Rebooting Project in October 2017, but she left the show on November 24 due to health issues.


== Personal life ==
Cha was born in Gwangju, and is the younger sister of Baro, a former member of B1A4.
