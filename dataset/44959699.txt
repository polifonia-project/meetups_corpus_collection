Frida Erika Appelgren, earlier married Muranius, born 5 April 1981 in Malmö, Sweden, is a Swedish music artist scoring a mid-2007 hit with the song Dunka mig gul och blå.Together with Headline she participated at Melodifestivalen 2008 with the song Upp o hoppa, which ended up 10th in the finals.
