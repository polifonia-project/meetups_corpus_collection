Mayumi Kameda is a Japanese pianist.
Trained at the Tōhō Gakuen Daigaku, she completed her studies at the Conservatoire de Paris, with teacher Gabriel Tacchino. Kameda won the 1981 Epinal Competition and was awarded the 1983 Cleveland International Piano Competition's 2nd award.
She remains internationally active as a concert pianist in a duo with Jean-Jacques Balet.
