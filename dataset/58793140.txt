Alex Herron is a Norwegian music video director and photographer. He has directed over 100 music videos and has also been a jury in Alarmprisen. Herron was born in a family of filmmakers and artists, and began his career at a young age. In 2003, he moved to Los Angeles to improve his skills, working on commercials and music videos.Herron was presented with the Spellemannprisen award for Margaret Berger's music video of "Lifetime Guarantee".
